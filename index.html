Welcome to your Study Planner!
This is your personal tool for staying on top of classes, assignments, and study sessions. Let's take a quick tour.
📚
Subjects
Go to Subjects to create your classes manually, or sync directly from Canvas to pull in your real courses and assignments automatically.
✅
Assignments
Each subject has its own assignment list. Add them manually or import from Canvas. Check them off as you complete them — the progress bar fills up.
⏱
Focus Timer
The Timer uses the Pomodoro technique — 25 min focus, then a short break. Pick a subject and your session gets logged automatically.
🎉
You're all set!
Start by going to Subjects and creating your first subject or syncing with Canvas. Replay this tutorial anytime from Settings.
